Ares Speed and Sound are located at:

764 Beaudesert Road, Coopers Plains, Brisbane, QLD, 4108.

  1. From the city simply take the South-East freeway and exit at the Mt Gravatt - Klump Road exit.
  2. Turn right and travel approx. 500 metres up Mains Road.
  3. Next, turn right into Kessles Road. Continue down Kessles road for approx. 1 kilometre. Kessles Road turns into Riawena.
  4. Continue along Riawena for a further 500+ metres and then turn left into Beaudesert Road.

Ares Speed and Sound should then be on your left.
