Ares Speed are an award winning, Brisbane based automotive enhancement business.
We are determined in helping our clients achieve their automotive dreams, by building the most stylish and unique cars on the road.

We are committed to leading the Queensland car culture by combining the latest trends & technology with our innovation and craftsmanship; we aim to produce unique machines that complement our clients personalities and desires.

With an established network of Australias most respected & well known automotive contacts, we can build an automobile with cutting edge street appeal and the performance to match.



DESIGN & AUTOSTYLING

We strive for aero styling at its finest in our designs and products. With hands on, in depth research and development we evolve concepts to fine works of automotive art. Our designs must conform to three main areas

FORM, FUNCTION & INNOVATION

  • Our designs must have shape and meet the contours of the car, and complement its original styling.
  • The aero kits must be shaped to enhance the appearance and the function of the car.
  • Design and research must be a vital part of the cars aero kit. Uniqueness and individuality also fall under our design theories

With the amalgamation of these design theories, we strive to create aero kits that have cutting edge designs, which complement the car body form, and ultimately enhance the cars aerodynamic function.

POWER & PERFORMANCE

As mentioned earlier, we have an established network of national and international suppliers, of the premium performance brands and tuning houses from across the globe. These connections provide us with the established research and development and potential to create purpose built cars to suit all racing applications, from street performance, to drag, and circuit racing.

In addition to the larger suppliers of performance products and experience, we have applied our own ingenuity with consultation of our race engineers and designers. With designers and engineers at the forefront of technology, our R&D team has developed simple and effective products with the aid of CAD and CNC technologies.
